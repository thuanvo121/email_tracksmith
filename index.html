The world can be messy. Make mud and meltwater the least of your worries. Drawing inspiration from the snug comfort and protection of wetsuits, our Thaw Tights and Half Tights are built to weather the slushiest days of shoulder season, so you can splash through puddles with toddler-like abandon.
Shop Tights
Two large envelope back pockets allow for easy storage of gloves, hat or gels. A zippered pocket on the right hip securely fits most smartphones.
We designed the tights with a high, wide waistband for maximum comfort. Plus, zippers at the back cuffs make it easy to pull them on and off.
For warmth, we used a unique two-sided fabric with plenty of stretch. It’s finished with an eco-dry water and stain-repelling treatment to keep you protected over the course of a messy run.
Our Thaw Collection was inspired by Vermont’s Mud Season. On the hunt for less messy exports from the Green Mountain State, we teamed up with Drink Simple to craft a limited-edition version of their sparkling maple water. Drink Simple harnesses the natural infusion power of trees, to create delicious and nutritious maple water naturally full of phytonutrients.
